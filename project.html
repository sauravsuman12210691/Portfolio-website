Mock-Master

Developed a web application, MockMaster, using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) integrated with generative AI to facilitate mock interviews and provide ATS (Applicant Tracking System) scores.

  • AI-Driven Question Generation

    Implemented generative AI to generate industry-standard questions tailored to the user's skills and projects.

  • Real-Time Interview Simulation

    Designed the system to require immediate responses to displayed questions, simulating real-time interview conditions.

  • Automated Scoring and Feedback

    Integrated functionality for recording answers, providing scores, and offering detailed reviews based on the user's responses.
